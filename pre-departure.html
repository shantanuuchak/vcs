Pre-Departure Guidance

Visa Consultancy Services helps you to , Travel, & Settle abroad. We provide an insight to various facets of a students’ life in a foreign land. We provide an opportunity for the students, & his/her family members to interact with other students of VCS who are already residing in abroad, so they can get detailed information on what to look forward to.

With our pre-departure guidance, we give students with lots of useful information and insights that will help them in settling down in the foreign country. Before students depart to the new country, we hold a pre-departure session where our team of experienced counselors shares some valuable insights about several aspects of student’s life.

we provide information about student life in foreign countries, facts about how to deal with homesickness and adaptability to the new culture. In addition, we even provide an opportunity to meet other students and their parents who are residing abroad.

The valuable session of pre-departure throws light on formalities completed at Indian and International airport like introduction to students flying to the same country/university, familiarization with country’s culture, college. It’s study pattern, and details of airport pick up and accommodation arrangements, etc.
