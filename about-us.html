About VCS

“VISA CONSULTANCY SERVICES”. We believe in enriching lives of each & every student who approaches us, beyond his /her admission needs at a reputed University. This is achieved by our very personalized one on one interaction with students and their families. We also stay connected with students post their admissions, ensuring that each student can go confidently in the direction and helping their dream come true.

Our History

Visa Global Education is the pioneer in overseas education consultancy since 2002. With quality credentials and our trustworthy service guides students to reach the best possible academic heights based on their academic merit and financial capacity.

Vision & Mission

Facilitating best educational opportunities in the prestigious universities abroad, We have about 1000 plus students to our credit, who have been placed in top universities all over the world. We are the acknowledged leader in overseas educational consultancy in Hyderabad, Telangane.

Achievements

Complementing the aptitudes of students we recommend nothing less than the best in their academic pursuit which in turn places them in lucrative jobs at MNCs. Relieving you of uncertainties and confusion regarding the career options, we can serve you by providing the best counselling and guidance to help you make the right decision.
